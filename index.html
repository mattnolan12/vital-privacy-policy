Vital - Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Introduction

Vital respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. By using Vital, you agree to the terms described here.

Information We Collect

Vital does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. Workout data and preferences are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Data

Data within the app is used only to provide you with personalized features such as workout tracking, progress visualization, and exercise recommendations. No data is sent to external servers unless otherwise stated in a future update.

Third-Party Services

Currently, Vital does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services. If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

Your Control

Since your data remains on your device, you have full control. You may delete the app at any time to remove all stored data.

Children’s Privacy

Vital is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected here with a revised “Last updated” date.
