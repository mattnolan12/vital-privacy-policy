Vital - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Introduction

Vital respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is stored, and your rights as a user. By using Vital, you agree to the terms described here.

What We Collect

Vital collects only the information you provide directly:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weight)
  • Nutrition entries (meals, macros)
  • Your fitness preferences and training frequency

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Health data from Apple Health or Google Fit
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports beyond what Apple provides

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Vital does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

Third-Party Services

Vital uses the following third-party service:

  • Apple App Store — processes subscription payments for Vital Pro. We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription status. RevenueCat may receive a randomized App User ID to validate your subscription. No personally identifiable information is shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Subscription

Vital Pro is available as a monthly subscription at $6.99/month after a 7-day free trial. Payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. You can cancel anytime through your App Store subscription settings. No charges are made during the free trial period.

Children's Privacy

Vital is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Your Control

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You may delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected here with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
